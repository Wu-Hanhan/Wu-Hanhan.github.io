**Spring 2025** — Completed the *Inclusive Teaching for STEM Graduate Students Mini-Course* offered by the Stearns Center for Teaching and Learning at George Mason University.

This program focuses on evidence-based strategies for creating inclusive and equitable learning environments in STEM disciplines. Topics covered include universal design for learning, culturally responsive pedagogy, and techniques for supporting diverse student populations in the classroom.
