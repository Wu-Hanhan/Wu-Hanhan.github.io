**Spring 2026** — I serve as the Recitation Instructor for ECE 528: Introduction to Random Processes, a graduate-level course in the ECE department at George Mason University.

- Lead two weekly 75-minute recitation sections: one in-person and one online via Zoom.
- Reinforce lecture material by working through problem sets, proofs, and applied examples in probability theory and stochastic processes.
- Support student learning through interactive problem-solving sessions and real-time Q&A in both modalities.
